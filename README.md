# Dark Elegant Site — Starter

A minimal dark, elegant website scaffold you can edit.

## Files
- `index.html` — main markup (hero, features, work, contact)
- `styles.css` — all styling, variables at the top for easy theming
- `script.js` — small JS for year injection and theme toggle

## Usage
1. Open `index.html` in your browser.
2. Edit copy, colors (CSS variables), or components as needed.
3. Replace placeholder content (Project A, etc.) with your content or components.

## Suggestions for customization
- Change color variables at the top of `styles.css` to tweak palette.
- Add your logo (replace the `.brand` text).
- Replace `work-item` boxes with images/cards and links.
- Swap in your preferred font(s) in the `<head>` of `index.html`.
- Add a contact form or integrate with a backend / static form service.
